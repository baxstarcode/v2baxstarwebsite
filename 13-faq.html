What makes Baxstar different from other guides? +

Three things: every trip is private (your group only, never shared boats), Brady runs tournament-grade equipment on every trip — not as an upgrade, and 30 years of local pattern knowledge means he's not guessing where the fish are. Most guides have one of these. Baxstar has all three.

What should I bring? +

Weather-appropriate clothing, sunscreen, a cooler for beverages, and your Minnesota fishing license. That's it. All fishing equipment, bait, tackle, and safety gear is provided. Brady runs a fully outfitted operation — you don't need a single piece of fishing gear.

Do I need a fishing license? +

Yes — Minnesota requires a valid fishing license for anyone 16 and older. You can purchase one online at the Minnesota DNR website in about two minutes. Brady can walk you through the process if needed.

How many people can fish per boat? +

Up to 4 guests per boat, comfortably. For larger groups — corporate retreats, family reunions, bachelor parties — Brady coordinates multiple boats with additional guides so every guest gets the full Baxstar experience. Group inquiries are welcome.

What if the weather is bad? +

Light rain produces some of the best fishing days of the season. Dangerous conditions — lightning, high winds, severe storms — Brady will contact you to reschedule at no charge. Safety is never compromised.

Is this the right experience for beginners? +

Completely. Brady calibrates every trip to your experience level. First-timers receive hands-on coaching and patient instruction throughout the day. Experienced anglers get pushed to new water and more advanced techniques. The goal is the same: everyone catches fish.
