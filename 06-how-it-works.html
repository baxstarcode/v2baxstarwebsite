The Process

HOW IT WORKS.

From reservation to the moment you leave the dock, Baxstar handles every detail. Your job is to show up and focus on the fish.

01
Reserve Your Date

Book online through FareHarbor in under two minutes — or call Brady directly if you have questions or want to discuss a custom trip. Your deposit holds the boat and locks your date. Prime summer weekends book 60 to 90 days out.

02
We Handle Everything

Brady plans the route based on current lake conditions, scouting reports, and weather. The boat is fueled, rigged, and ready. Live bait is sourced fresh. All tackle and safety equipment is prepared. You receive a trip confirmation with everything you need to know.

03
You Focus on the Fish

Arrive at the dock. Everything is ready. Brady runs the operation — positioning the boat, reading the electronics, coaching technique, and adapting when conditions shift. Your catch is cleaned, vacuum-packed, and photos are delivered same day. All you need to bring is a cooler and a license.
